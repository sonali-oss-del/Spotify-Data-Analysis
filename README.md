📌 Project Overview

This project presents an interactive Spotify Music Listening Analytics Dashboard developed using Microsoft Power BI. The dashboard transforms Spotify streaming history data into meaningful insights, enabling detailed analysis of listening behavior, music preferences, device usage, artist popularity, track engagement, and listening patterns over time.

The goal of this project is to analyze personal music consumption trends and provide data-driven insights into how, when, and what users listen to most frequently on Spotify.

🎯 Project Objectives
Analyze overall Spotify listening activity over different years.
Understand trends in albums, artists, and tracks played over time.
Identify most listened albums, artists, and tracks.
Compare listening habits between weekdays and weekends.
Analyze device preferences for music streaming.
Study the impact of shuffle and track skipping behavior.
Discover peak listening hours and daily listening patterns.
Explore relationships between average listening duration and track frequency.

Provides a high-level summary of Spotify listening activity through interactive KPIs and trend analysis.

🎵 Total Albums Played
🎤 Total Artists Played
🎧 Total Tracks Played
📅 Year-over-Year (YoY) listening comparison

Music Consumption Trends

The dashboard analyzes yearly changes in listening behavior:

Album listening trend over years.
Artist listening trend over years.
Track listening trend over years.
Comparison of current year performance with previous years.
Top Music Preferences

Provides ranking of the most frequently played:

Top 5 Albums by total play count.
Top 5 Artists by total play count.
Top 5 Tracks by total play count.

This helps identify the user's most preferred music content.

Device & Playback Behavior Analysis

Interactive filters allow analysis based on:

Android
iOS
Web Player
Windows
Mac
Cast Devices

Additional playback behavior filters:

Shuffle status (Shuffled / Not Shuffled)
Track skip status (Skipped / Not Skipped)

These filters provide deeper insights into how users interact with Spotify across different platforms.

2. Listening Pattern Analysis Dashboard

Provides a detailed view of daily and hourly listening habits.

Listening Hour Heatmap

The heatmap visualizes listening frequency based on:

Hour of the day
Day of the week

This helps identify peak listening periods and user engagement patterns.

Peak Listening Time Analysis

Analyzes the hours when music streaming activity is highest, helping discover:

Most active listening hours.
Differences between weekday and weekend listening habits.
Listening Duration vs Track Frequency Analysis

A scatter plot is used to understand the relationship between:

Average listening time per track.
Number of times tracks were played.

Interactive parameters allow users to dynamically filter:

Minimum average listening duration.
Minimum track frequency.

📈 Key Insights Generated
User listening activity has shown noticeable growth over the years.
A small group of albums, artists, and tracks contribute significantly to total plays.
Listening behavior varies across weekdays and weekends.
Specific hours of the day show significantly higher streaming activity.
Device preferences influence how and where users consume music.
Shuffle and skipping patterns reveal user engagement with tracks.
